Mehran Goli is a Senior Design Engineer at Socionext Europe. He previously worked as a Senior Researcher at the University of Bremen and the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI) in Bremen, Germany. He received a B.Sc. degree in computer engineering from the University of Shahid Beheshti, Tehran, Iran, in 2012, and M.Sc. degree in computer engineering from the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the University of Tehran (UT), Tehran, Iran, in 2015.
